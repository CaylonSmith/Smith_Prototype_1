Data Visualisation

Data visualization is the representation of quantitative data and information in a graphical context using pictorial elements such as Tables, charts, graphs, trees, scatter plots, diagrams, and maps (brush & Burns, 2020) (Li, 2020). Data visualization aids human cognition and provides new insights while also teaching the reader about said information.
