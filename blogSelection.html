Accessibility and its importance

Accessibility refers to the ability or lack of ability a product or service has to be able to be used by everyone who encounters said product or service no matter how they encounter it. Speaking within a more specific relation to this context, accessibility can also mean how well a design maximizes equal access to digital interfaces for everyone in a society.
