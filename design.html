Style_Guide

The following is my style guide.i started by creating a mood board, i used the mood board to dictate my typography and pallette.

Mood board

In order to create my mood board and set my aesthetic, i went to pinterest and sat there for abit looking up designs and illustrations that inspired me.Most of my favourite ones came from asian themed designs.I then grouped the ones i most liked together.They gave me a retro old timey feel hence most of my website i tried to keep that in mind.



My main inspiration for this font was its old aestheticand feel, also it was one of the fonts W3 schools recommended as the best web safe fonts.

This pallete was mainly inspired by one of the artworks in my mood board.These colours were simple and not used as much compared to colours like red ,blue etc.In order to make sure my website is not all over the place i used the 60-30-10 Colour rule so the beige colour would take most of the website as its least distracting and the orange would be the 10 percent as it is very bright , it would only be used to draw the users eyes to important things.

Reflection
